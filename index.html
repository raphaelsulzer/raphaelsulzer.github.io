Raphael Sulzer

73 avenue de Paris · 94165 Saint-Mandé CEDEX · RaphaelSulzer@gmx.de

I am a fourth year PhD student in the LaSTIG lab at the French National Mapping Agency (IGN) graduating in summer 2022. I am also part of the IMAGINE group at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC). The topic of my PhD is "Deep Surface Reconstruction from Point Clouds with Visibility Information". My advisors are Loic Landrieu, Renaud Marlet and Bruno Vallet. My research interests are centered around the fields of computer vision and computational geometry. I graduated from Delft University of Technology with a MSc in Geomatics (cum laude) and from the University of Stuttgart with a BSc in Geodesy and Geoinformatics.
