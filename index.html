Raphael Sulzer

73 avenue de Paris · 94165 Saint-Mandé CEDEX · Raphael.Sulzer@ign.fr

I am a second year PhD student in the LaSTIG lab at the French National Mapping Agency (IGN). I am also part of the IMAGINE group at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech (ENPC).

My research interests are centered around the fields of computational geometry, 3D machine learning and 3D GIS. My advisors are Bruno Vallet, Renaud Marlet and Loic Landrieu. I graduated from Delft University of Technology with a MSc in Geomatics and from the University of Stuttgart with a BSc in Geodesy and Geoinformatics.
